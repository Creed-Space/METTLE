Frequently Asked Questions

How does session counting work?

A session is counted when you call /api/session/start. Subsequent challenge answers and result retrieval don't count toward your limit. Sessions reset daily at midnight UTC.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

You'll receive a 429 (Too Many Requests) response. Upgrade to Pro or contact us for Enterprise to increase your limit.

Can I use METTLE for commercial applications?

Yes! All tiers allow commercial use. The Free tier is great for prototyping, Pro for production, and Enterprise for high-volume applications.

What's included in Enterprise support?

Enterprise includes dedicated account management, priority bug fixes, custom challenge development, and optional on-premise deployment assistance.

Is there a self-hosted option?

Yes, METTLE is open source under MIT license. You can self-host for free. Enterprise customers get deployment support and custom configurations.

How do webhooks work?

Register a webhook URL and we'll POST events (session.completed, badge.issued, badge.revoked) to your server. All payloads are HMAC-signed if you provide a secret.
